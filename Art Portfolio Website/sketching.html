Ink Pen Sketching

When it comes to actual drawing, my favorite medium is sketching with ink pen. I usually just scribble a bunch on the page to start and then go in and draw whatever I'm feeling. My favorite thing to sketch is probably my own face because it's what I'm best at. But, even then it doesn't fully look like me. I don't really sketch as much anymore just because I've been more focused on other things. So, these are mostly from a few years ago.
